    ABOUT ME

    Student in UX Design

    My passion for digital media has led me to pursue a career in Media. Currently I am studying for a bachelors in ICT & Media Design, as well as doing freelance designs on the side. Shown in many of my designs I also have an involvement in motorsports. Being able to combine both of these passions allows me to use different skills sets needed in each industry and combine them into one. I thrive off working with other people and delivering a product that exceeds the client’s expectations. My main designs so far have been liveries and logos, in addition I have successfully created other media content such as broadcasting and advertising material.
